WHAT WE DO
 The primary role of the Directorate is in training support while its secondary role is in welfare support.
Personnel Training Support
The Directorate is firmly driven towards achieving its primary objectives of professionally equiping officers and men of the NN.
Nigerian Navy Military School
The Nigerian Navy Military School, Ikot Ntuen, the first and only Military School established by the Nigerian Navy commenced both military, and academic activities on 15 April 2018.
Nigerian Navy Secondary Schools
Since 1982, the NN has established outstanding Secondary Schools . There are currently 7 NN Secondary Schools across the country to service both children of personnel and civilians
Nigerian Navy Primary Schools
In accordance to the NN policy, every Naval Base/unit in the country inhabits an NN primary school to service the wards of its personnel and civilian staff.
NOWA Schools
The Directorate of Naval Education provides policy and advisory roles to Naval Officers Wives Association schools.
